# Rubrics

## Universal rubric (applies to most artifacts)

### 1) Clarity (0–5)
- 0–1: unclear goals, vague claims
- 2–3: understandable but missing structure
- 4–5: crisp purpose, readable, well-scoped

### 2) Evidence & citations (0–5)
- 0–1: unsupported claims
- 2–3: some citations but gaps
- 4–5: strong primary sources; claims matched to evidence

### 3) Reproducibility (0–5)
- 0–1: not runnable; missing versions
- 2–3: partial instructions
- 4–5: pinned deps, seeds, rerunnable steps

### 4) Safety & limitations (0–5)
- 0–1: no discussion
- 2–3: generic disclaimer
- 4–5: concrete failure modes, guardrails, conservative design

### 5) Usefulness (0–5)
- 0–1: unclear application
- 2–3: plausible but untested
- 4–5: evaluated against realistic scenarios, actionable outcomes

## Score bands
- 20–25: publishable/portfolio-ready
- 14–19: solid but needs polish
- <14: revise scope, evidence, or reproducibility
